ChooseWell Help

Text reminders notify you of deadlines and your incentive status. If you received an error message, here are the possible reasons:

  • You sent an invalid CHORUS ID. To register for text reminders, reply with your CHORUS ID. (If you're a spouse or domestic partner of a Michelin employee, add "A" to end of the employee's CHORUS ID.)
  • Someone already used this CHORUS ID to sign up for text reminders. We only allow one account per CHORUS ID. (If you're a spouse or domestic partner of a Michelin employee, add "A" to end of the employee's CHORUS ID.)
  • Someone already used this phones to sign up for text reminders. Please register with another phone.
  • You sent a response that was not recognized. This phone is already registered for email reminders. Valid responses are STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for more info or LIKE.
  • Please try sending your message again. If you have questions, call the PSC (877) 435-7868.
