Three Ways to Play!

This game is all about earning points for what you do and find, and progressing through levels. There are three ways to tell us what you do: the play box online, or text/email us... you pick what works best for you. Here are some examples of ways to earn points:

1. Earn Activity Points

You can earn points for eating healthy and for doing healthy activities.
Here are some examples:
Eating healthy:
  • Apple: Send “ate apple” (+3 points)
  • Salad: Send “ate salad” (+3 points)
  • Water: Send “drank water” (+1 point)
Doing physical activity:
  • Took the stairs: Send “took stairs” (+2 points)
  • Went to the gym: Send “went to gym” (+10 points)

2. Earn Tile Points

Look for the fun, colorful squares around your office. Read them and follow the instructions on each one.

3. Earn Social Points

Games are more fun when you’re playing with other people!
  • Refer coworkers. Get 5 points for each coworker you sign up: have your friend respond to their welcome text or email with your user name.
  • Play together. You can earn extra points by doing an activity with a coworker by adding their user name to the end of a command (“took stairs jsmith”) or having them add your user name to the end of a command.
  • Become a fan. No points, but a great way to check out what your colleagues are up to. Search for them on the “Directory” page and click “follow”. Or text or email “follow” followed by their username (i.e., “follow jsmith”).
