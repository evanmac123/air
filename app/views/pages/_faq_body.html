Basics

What is H Engage?

H Engage is a social, mobile game that makes getting healthy fun – and rewarding.

How do I play?

There are three categories of points: activity points, social points, and tile points (see How can I earn activity points?↓, How can I earn social points?↓, and How can I earn tile points?↓ for more information).
You can earn points for any of these categories, by sending commands to H Engage via text or email, or submitting them online.

Does it cost anything to play?

There is no cost to join, but standard message and data rates do apply so there may be some costs associated with the use of this service. For example, your pager or cellular phone service provider may charge you to send or receive wireless text messages.

Where can I find my user name?

Your user name was listed in the welcome message you received when you first signed up for the game. Can’t find the welcome message? Text in “MYID” or take a look at the “Settings” page.

What if I don’t want to play by text messaging?

Text messaging makes the game fun, but it’s certainly not required.
You can play by sending commands as an email, or by submitting them on hengage.com
If you’ve already entered your mobile number but want to remove it, simply go to hengage.com, click on “Settings” and remove your mobile number.

What can I do on hengage.com?

  • You can watch your points add up, see other people’s game play and how they compare to you on the scoreboard
  • Connect with your coworkers. You can also do this by sending “connect USER NAME” (e.g., “Connect jsmith”) as a text or email. Your coworker will be notified and asked to confirm that they know you.

What information about me can people see on hengage.com?

We get that privacy is important. That’s why we let you customize your privacy settings on hengage.com. You have the option to show your profile page to all coworkers who are playing the game, only coworkers who you are connected to, or nobody.
If you limit your profile page to those you are connected to or nobody, others will only be able to see your name, photo, and email address.
If a coworker wants to connect with you, they will request to be your fan. You will receive a notification when a coworker requests to be your fan, and will have the option to confirm or (quietly) reject them.

The Rules of the Game

Where do I enter commands?

You can play by sending commands as a text message or an email, or by submitting them on hengage.com

What’s a command?

A command is what you send to H Engage to earn points. It lets us know that you did a healthy activity (like taking a walk or eating an apple), saw a tile or did an activity with a coworker.
Here’s how they work:
  • Commands for eating healthy foods and doing healthy activities are pretty self-explanatory… eat an apple, send “ate apple,” take a walk, text “took walk.” See How can I earn activity points?↓ for more information.
  • Tile commands are based on information you can find right on the tiles, so no guesswork there.
  • Social commands involve sending a coworker’s username to H Engage so we know you did an activity together (i.e., “took walk jsmith”). See How can I earn social points?↓ for more information about how to send social commands.

What if the system doesn’t recognize a command?

There are a few reasons that the system may not recognize a command that you send in. First, you may want to double check what you sent us. For example, make sure that you spelled everything correctly (sent “benefit” not “bnefit”) and that words are in the right order (“4 ur health” not “ur 4 health”).
If you’re sending in an activity command, there’s a chance that you did a healthy activity that we haven’t loaded into our system yet. We think we’ve covered all the common – and not so common – activities and healthy foods, but if you find one the system doesn’t recognize, you can send “S” to suggest we add it.
And finally, if you’re still having issues, send support@hengage.com a message and we’ll look into it.

What if I send a command and don’t receive a response back?

We try our best to respond to all commands within a few seconds. Like any system, ours slows down once in awhile so if you don’t see something right away, give us a few minutes. If you still don’t see a response within an hour, send us a note at support@hengage.com and we’ll look into it.

How can I earn activity points?

You can earn points for eating healthy and for doing healthy activities. We think we’ve covered all the common – and not so common – activities and healthy foods, but if you find one the system doesn’t recognize, you can send “S” to suggest we add it.
Here are some examples of healthy commands:
Eating healthy
Tell H Engage when you eat healthy foods, like:
Fruit
  • Apple: Send “ate apple” (+3 points)
  • Banana: Send “ate banana” (+3 points)
  • Strawberries: Send “ate strawberry” (+3 points)
Vegetables
  • Salad: Send “ate salad” (+3 points)
  • Broccoli: Send “ate broccoli” (+3 points)
  • Bok choy: Send “ate bok choy” (+3 points)
Other
  • Whole grains: Send “ate whole grains” (+3 points)
  • Almonds: Send “ate almonds” (+3 points)
  • Peanuts: Send “ate peanuts” (+3 points)
  • Walnuts: “ate walnuts” (+3 points)
  • Water: Send “drank water” (+1 point)
Doing physical activity.
Tell H Engage when you do healthy activities, like:
  • Took the stairs: Send “took stairs” (+2 points)
  • Went to the gym: Send “went to gym” (+10 points)
  • Took a walk: Send “took walk” (+5 points)
  • Went canoeing: Send “went canoeing” (+6 points)
  • Played doubles tennis: Send “I played tennis” (+8 points)

How can I earn tile points?

Find and read “tiles”. Look for the fun, colorful squares. Read them and follow the instructions on each one…have your cell phone ready!
Tiles will typically include one or more of the following ways to earn points:
  • ✔ Check-in: Let us know you found the tile by sending the command in this box
  • ? Answer a question: Answer a question based on the tile or on the information you receive back from checking-in
  • ★ Make a commitment: Commit to taking an action in the future

How can I tell if I’ve found all the tiles?

We number the tiles in a series to make it easier to find them all. So for example, the tile you’re looking at may have “1/4” in the upper right corner. This means you’ve found the first of four in the series. To make it easier, when you “check-in” to a tile, (see How can I earn tile points?↑ for more information), the response you receive back will tell you that you’ve found one out of four, two out of four, etc.

How can I tell what Level I am?

You can find your Level (and how many points you need to get to the next level) on your homepage on hengage.com.
You’ll also receive an update on your points when you send in a command.

How can I find out how many points I have?

The easiest way to find out how many points you have is to log on to hengage.com. You’ll be able to see what Level you’re in and how many points you need to level-up right at the top of your homepage.
You’ll also receive an update on your points when you send in a command.

Playing with Friends

How can I earn social points?

We all know that games are more fun when you’re playing with other people. That’s why H Engage gives you two ways to earn points for playing with your coworkers:
  • Referring coworkers. Get 5 points for each coworker you sign up: have your friend respond to their welcome text or email with your user name.
  • Playing together. You can earn extra points by doing an activity with a coworker by adding their user name to the end of a command (“took stairs jsmith”) or having them add your user name to the end of a command.

How do I connect with a coworker on H Engage?

Connecting with a coworker is a great way to support their healthy activities. There are two ways to become a fan of a coworker:
  • You can text or email “connect” followed by their username (i.e., “connect jsmith”)
  • Or you can go to hengage.com, look them up in the “Directory” page, click their name to go to their profile page, and you’ll see a button that you can click to follow them.
Either way, the person will be notified and asked to confirm that they know you.

How do I refer someone to the game?

You can earn points for getting your coworkers to join the game. Just have them sign up for the game (the same way you did) and then ask them to respond to their welcome text or email with your user name.

A friend referred me to the game. How do I give them credit?

There are a few ways to give your referrer credit. The easiest is to respond to the welcome message you received when you joined with their user name. You can also give them credit when you log on to hengage.com.

A friend told me about a command. How do I give them credit?

You can give a friend credit for telling you about a command (or for doing an activity with you) by adding their user name to the end of the command when you send it in. Here are some examples:
  • Took a walk with a coworker? Send “took a walk jsmith”
  • Ate an apple with a coworker? Send “ate apple jsmith”
